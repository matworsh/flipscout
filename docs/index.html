FAQ

How do I activate? Enter your sub_ license key in the extension settings page.
Will it always work? FlipScout reads public sold listings data directly in your browser, so it keeps working even when APIs change.
How do I cancel? You can cancel from your Stripe customer portal link in your receipt email.
Privacy: FlipScout stores your usage count and license key locally in your browser. We do not collect or transmit personal data.
